To buy or sell

Simply and at the best price, with 1Inch :  https://app.1inch.io/#/100/advanced/swap/USDC/REG

  1. Connect your wallet If you have a ReaToken Wallet, you must go through WalletConnectThe wheel, at the top right, allows you to change the language.
  2. Choose the payment currency (USDC on the image, but there are many others.. Including armmToken and EURe (from a purchase in Euro with Monerium)
  3. And, make the two approvals (approve, then swap)
     

To sell it, you just need to reverse the direction of the swap with the small central arrow.
