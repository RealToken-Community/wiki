Organization

The DAO is managed by RealT for its beginnings. The DAO members are gradually taking over the responsibilities delegated to RealT, notably through the following Committees:

  • Proposal: 
    To assist in establishing proposals on the Forum and corresponding votes on Tally (onChain).
     Members: Sigri, DRKTT, and PhilP
  • Security Committee: 
    To ensure the security of the DAO protocols (at a minimum, intervene urgently to stop a protocol). 
    Members: Benoist-Not-for-sale, Cogatos, Sigri, EzSwim, JoeD
  • Voting Power Establishment Working Group:
    To study and propose new versions of the Voting Power calculation algorithm based on the REGs held. 
    Members: Taodiem, Paul, PhilP, Yohann76, Eric, Battistu, DRKTT, Stouban, Teugos
  • Liquidity Management Committee: 
    To manage the liquidity strategy implemented via [RIP00003], study and propose new liquidity management strategies. Liquidity. 
    Members: Teugos, PhilP
  • Marketing and Communications Committee: 
    To study and propose marketing and communications initiatives to increase visibility and engagement within the Realtoken ecosystem. 
    Members: Currently being elected.
