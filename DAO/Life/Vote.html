Organization

The DAO is managed by RealT for its beginnings. A proposal (Authority framework) specifies the scope of responsibility of RealT and the principle of the progressive transfer to the DAO: vote to come. The decentralization of responsibilities (currently handled by RealT) has already begun, through elections of members for specific missions:

  • Proposer
    To help establish: proposals on the Forum and corresponding votes on Tally (onChain). Proposal in principle voted (RIP00005), Application and election: in progress
  • Security Committee
    To ensure the security of the DAO protocols (at a minimum, intervene urgently to stop a protocol). Proposal in principle voted (RIP00006). Mode of operation and election: ongoing
  • Working group for the establishment of Voting Powers
    To study and propose the next version of the algorithm that calculates Voting Power from the REG held.Proposal in principle voted (RIP00007). Application and election: ongoing
  • Liquidity Management Committee
    On the occasion of RIP00003 (creation of a Sushiswap v3 pool) the principle of a committee for the management of positions was voted. Its election will be the subject of a future proposal.

Actions, currently carried out by RealT on RMM, will now be decided by the DAO (RIP00008).
