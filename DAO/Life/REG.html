Creation of REGs

The DAO governance tokens (RealToken Ecosystem Governance, REG) are created at two times:

  • When the DAO is created (2024), for a fixed quantity of 500 million and divided into 8 budgets.
  • During the first revaluation of a Realtoken:
    • USDREG ($ convertible to REG) is allocated to holders of the revalued Realtoken.
    • These USDREG are convertible into REG, which is created at that time (this is the "inflation" of the REG quantity).
    • Before the emergence of USDREG and the conversion application, SOON tokens were distributed. They have since disappeared.
    • For more details: link

The quantity of REG may increase or decrease, depending on the decisions of the DAO.

First Distribution (Airdrop Genesis)

All RealT client wallets (KYC) could request, during the month of May 2024, 100 REG for free per wallet. 
The objective of this distribution was to establish a first price level of REG. 
2,061,500 REG were minted on this occasion and deposited in a Vault.
769,200 REG were claimed (37%), on the corresponding application, during the distribution month. 
The price was set at just under $1. 
This airdrop being taken from the DAO Treasury budget, the 1,298,600 unclaimed REG were transferred to the Treasury Safe wallet (10/23/2024).
