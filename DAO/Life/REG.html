Creation of REGs

The DAO governance tokens (RealToken Ecosystem Governance, REG) are created at two times:

  • When the DAO is created (2024), for a fixed quantity of 500 million and divided into 8 budgets.
  • During the first revaluation of a Realtoken:
    • Before the creation of the REG, SOON tokens (*) were created, which will soon be convertible into REG.
    • Once the Soon have disappeared, the REG will be created and allocated to the owner of the Realtoken at the time of its first revaluation. The distribution will be done in accordance with the voted procedure (RIP00009).

The quantity of REG may increase or decrease, depending on the decisions of the DAO.

(*): Quantity and holders of Soon (non-transferable token, unlike the REG) : link

First Distribution (Airdrop Genesis)

All RealT client wallets (KYC) could request, during the month of May 2024, 100 REG for free per wallet. 
The objective of this distribution was to establish a first price level of REG. 
2,061,500 REG were minted on this occasion and deposited in a Vault.
769,200 REG were claimed (37%), on the corresponding application, during the distribution month. 
The price was set at just under $1. 
This airdrop being taken from the DAO Treasury budget, the 1,298,600 unclaimed REG were transferred to the Treasury Safe wallet (10/23/2024).

RMM Deposit Token (armmV3..)


RMM deposit tokens come from the revenue generated by RMM v3. RMM charges 10% fees on the interest on loans (in USDC and WXDAI). These fees are shared between: AAVE (the basis of the RMM application) for 20%; and the DAO for 80%.

The DAO share is received on the Treasury in RMM deposit tokens (so they grow, as long as they are not converted to stablecoin). 

In 2024, they were collected (from RMM to the Treasury) twice in October and December.

 

Two complementary movements took place:

  • On November 8, armmv3USDC were converted into USDC to supply the 6000 USDC of the first incentive epoch (RIP00001).
  • On November 15, the amount that had been collected (and which grew) by a first preDAO initiative in 2022 (SGOV) was paid into the DAO treasury.

The amount displayed above for each of the two RMM deposit tokens includes the interest related to the deposits.
